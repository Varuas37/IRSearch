Head of state and of government of the U.S. state of Texas

The governor of Texas heads the state government of Texas. The governor is the chief authority of the executive and legislative branch of the state government and is the commander in chief of the Texas National Guard. The current governor is Greg Abbott, who took office in 2015. Wikipedia
